Robbert Baratheon

King Robert I Baratheon was the ruler of the Seven Kingdoms. He took the throne through conquest in the war known as Robert's Rebellion. Robert married Cersei Lannister to cement an alliance with House Lannister to hold the kingdoms together, but the marriage was loveless

Stannis Baratheon

King Stannis I Baratheon was the Lord of Dragonstone, the younger brother of King Robert Baratheon and older brother of Renly Baratheon

Renly Baratheon

King Renly I Baratheon was the youngest of three brothers and thus fourth in line to inherit the throne after Joffrey, Tommen, and Stannis. He was granted the title of Lord Paramount of the Stormlands by his brother Robert, but abandoned the latter position when laying claim to the Iron Throne.

Joffrey Baratheon

King Joffrey Baratheon was the second Baratheon king to sit on the Iron Throne. Though believed by most to be the eldest son of King Robert Baratheon and Queen Cersei Lannister, Joffrey is actually a bastard born from Cersei's incestuous relationship with her twin brother, Ser Jaime Lannister.

Tommen Baratheon

King Tommen Baratheon was the younger brother of King Joffrey. After his brother's death, Tommen assumed the throne. After learning of the death of his beloved Queen among all the casualties in the Great Sept of Baelor, Tommen committed suicide by jumping out of a window in the Red Keep

Myrcella Baratheon

Princess Myrcella Baratheon was commonly thought to be the only daughter of King Robert Baratheon and Queen Cersei Lannister. However, like her brothers, her real father is Jaime Lannister

Shireen Baratheon

Princess Shireen Baratheon was the daughter of Stannis and Selyse Baratheon.
